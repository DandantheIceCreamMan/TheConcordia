Our Mission

The Social Events Club exists to make campus feel smaller, friendlier, and more fun. We organize inclusive events where students can meet new people, try new things, and take a break from coursework.

Use this site to explore upcoming events, suggest ideas, vote in polls, and stay up to date through our newsletter.
