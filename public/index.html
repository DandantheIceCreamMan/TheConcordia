What We Do

The Social Events Club runs a full calendar of get-togethers for everyone on campus. We host mixers, game nights, movie screenings, outdoor hangouts, and one-off theme events so there’s always something to look forward to.

Whether you want to meet new people, try a new hobby, or just take a break from studying, our events are designed to be welcoming and low-pressure. We also listen to you: students can propose event ideas and vote in polls to shape what we do next.

Our Mission

The Social Events Club exists to make campus feel smaller, friendlier, and more fun. We believe that connection and belonging are essential to student life, and that a few good events can turn strangers into friends and the campus into a community.

We organize inclusive, low-barrier events where students can meet new people, try new things, and take a real break from coursework. Our goal is to give every student a place to show up as themselves and leave with at least one new connection or memory.

This website is your hub: browse upcoming events, suggest ideas for future events, vote in polls so we know what you want, and sign up for our newsletter so you never miss an announcement. We’re here to make your time at school more social and more fun.
